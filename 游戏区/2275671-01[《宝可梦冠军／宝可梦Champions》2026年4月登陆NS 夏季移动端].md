Pokémon Champions uses a new stat system for Pokémon.

- Pokémon always at level 50, 31 IVs.

- Up to 32 Stat Points (SPs) in any stat, 66 total.

- To convert to the old EV system, 1 SP = 8 EVs.

- And yes, for comparison purposes, if we think in terms of the old EV system, Pokémon can now have up to 256 EVs in any stat and 528 EVs total.
